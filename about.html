Who are we?

Map data, by Google Maps © 2020 Google

Café Papanui is a family-run business established in the Christchurch suburb of Papanui in 1989 by the husband and wife team of Waipatu and April Te Awa.

Waipatu and April met while attending a chef training course at Christchurch Polytech in the mid 1980's. After completing their studies they worked as chefs in London for several years.

Upon their return to Christchurch they realised their dream of opening their own café by establishing Café Papanui.

Their daughter Maia joined the business in 2016.
